About this experience

🔹 Description

This is a simple web-based AR experience that allows users to try interactive face lenses directly in their browser. It demonstrates creative augmented reality possibilities and my skills to imagine an augmented world.

🔹 Integration Description

The application integrates Snap Camera Kit Web SDK to show face lenses in real time. Camera Kit processes the video stream locally in the browser. Only my approved lenses are available. The experience is "Powered by Snap".

🔹 Privacy Policy

Video processing occurs locally within your browser. We do not store, record, or transmit any video data or personal data to our servers. The experience runs entirely in the user’s browser.
